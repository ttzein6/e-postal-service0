EMS

EMS is a Premium Postal Service that allows the external transfer of documents and goods in accordance with international standards within a period of 5 to 7 days with competitive prices.

Additional information

Thanks to this service, packages are delivered to anyone present at the destination address upon getting his signature without any additional charges. The size of the packages may vary between 0.25 and 30 kilograms. Tracking your mail is also possible on our website.
