Annual Residency Permit

With this service, Arabs and Foreigners residing in Lebanon may renew their annual residency permit through any LibanPost office. After processing the application with the competent authority, we then deliver the permit to the applicant's address and transfer the fees to the General Directory of General Security.

Apply
Additional information

The application can be lodged by the incumbent in person, or legal representative by virtue of a notarized proxy according to the category of the residency (first, second, third or fourth). 'First Class: Any family member of a first class work permit holder (husband/wife - underage kids) A work permit holder (category: house cook) A work permit holder in person Second Class: A work permit holder (category: assistant house cook) Any family member of a work permit holder (except for Egyptians) A work permit holder in person Third Class: Janitor, construction laborer, municipality worker, fisherman A worker in an institution (establishment) or a company Fourth Class: A domestic worker (Domestic Services)
