Postal Registered Mail

Incoming registered mail delivery services are special mail measures taken when the sender wishes to have an acknowledgement of receipt in the form of an official document that proves that the mail has been sent, delivered and received, in accordance with competent courts customary measures.

Additional information

This service applies to packages and parcels sent internally and abroad
